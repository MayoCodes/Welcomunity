Help

The website allows users to help others who are struggling refugees, by essentially listing in-person event in which users can participate in to raise money, food, clothes, or awareness for refugees and others in need

Map

The website allows users to access a map in which nearby areas are marked that help and support for refugees an others who are in need.

Profile

The website allows users to create and edit their own profile where they can show their, name, nationality, and bio. From this information, the website will try to connect profiles so they will have someone who can relate and trust.(this feature is still in development)

Support

The website allows users to reach out to the support team in case of any issues

Donating money

You should donate money to refugees because it can help them survive and rebuild their lives, and because it can benefit the economy and society of their host countries. Donating money is a way of showing kindness and support for people who have lost everything.

Donating Food

You should donate food to refugees because it can help them meet their nutrition needs and prevent hunger in times of crisis.

Donating Clothes

You should donate clothes to refugees because it can help them have access to affordable and appropriate clothing that can protect them from the weather, improve their hygiene and dignity, and support their integration into a new society

Becoming a Mentor

You should become a mentor to refugees because it can help them improve their well-being, self-confidence, motivation, education and protection in a new country.
